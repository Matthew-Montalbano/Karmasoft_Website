Sales Reports

Detailed and flexible sales reports. View sales report by date, product, workshop, coupon and more, Toggle betwen quantity and amount. Export data to CSV a excel compatible format.

Marketing reports are a great tool, if you can turn them into actionable metrics. With Karmasoft every marketing report is a focus email campaign. With one click you can sent an email with CTA links to every clients on the list.

Payroll Reports

Clear exportable payroll reports for your teachers/ hourly staffs and workshop. Set payroll based on attendance, fixed rate per class or per student or both. Export data to CSV file.
